# New York City Verticals Tasting

Friday, March 27, 2026
10:00am - 1:00pm ET
Sky Loft | 50 Hudson Yards, New York, NY 10001

One of La Paulée’s signature events, our Verticals Tasting is a unique opportunity to taste three-vintage verticals of a signature wine from participating domaines in the beautiful setting of Sky Loft Hudson Yards, featuring sweeping views of the NYC skyline.

There is no better way to explore different Burgundian vintages’ nuances or to discover how a single terroir’s expression can evolve over time \- and to do so with the grower who farmed the grapes and crafted the wine makes this a very special experience indeed.
